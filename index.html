🗣️ Behavioral Interview Prep
Every product company has at least 1 behavioral round. Amazon has 2. Candidates who prepare only DSA and skip behavioral get rejected in final rounds. Write these stories NOW — not the night before an interview.
STAR Format
SITUATION — Set context in 1-2 sentences. What project/team/constraint?
TASK — What was YOUR specific responsibility? What was the goal?
ACTION — What exact steps did YOU take? Use "I" not "we". Specific technical decisions and why.
RESULT — Measurable outcome. Numbers. Impact. Always quantify.
